Where can I have lunch/dinner?

Zaandam is a very diverse city from a gastronomic point of view, thus I can even compare it with Amsterdam. A lunch or dinner you want to have in Zaandam can take you anywhere on globe such as: Mexico, China, India, Greece, Italy, Japan or you can choose to eat at one of the McDonalds or Dominos fast foods. Most of restaurants are situated is the Old City so it is very easy to go to the restaurant and check their menu.
