Tourist attractions in Zaandam

Oostzijder Church

Originally the oldest church in Zaandam. The two-aisled hall church with a slender tower to the left. Various renovations, including the addition of a tower to the left of the church in 1850, designed by Zaan city architect LJ Immink. As a church of the Protestant municipality of Zaandam, it is only used very occasionally for worship services.

Sint-Bonifatius Church

The Saint Boniface Church in Zaandam is a neo-Gothic Roman Catholic church in Zaandam that was built between 1899 and 1900. It is characterized by a squat square tower and by a particularly cool and colorful neo-Gothic interior.

Zaanse Schans

One of the most famous attractions in Zaandam is the Zaanse Schans. As a short history for this place, Zaanse Schans is best known for its collection of windmills and wooden houses. At the Zaanse Schans you can find 5 working windmills, some of which are open to visit. These windmills perform different tasks, such as sawing wood, milling oil and grinding flour, spices and pigments in the same way they did twohundred years ago. When a windmill is open for visit, you can walk through the interior and take the rickety stairs all the way to the top deck, where you have to be careful not being hit by one of the sails!

There are also also activities you can do at Zaanse Schans like visiting:

Zaanse Tijd, which provides a charming and informative insight into the history of the Zaanse clock industry through beautiful Dutch timepieces.

Cheese Farm "Catharina Hoeve" the place where you can see the entire process of making dutch cheese and taste different types of cheese. In addition to that, you will find a wide range of delicious cheese specialties, for instance the most flavoursome of plain cheeses made from cow, goat and sheep milk.

Wooden Shoe Workshop where you can learn more about this craftmanship.
