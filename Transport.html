How can I go in Zaandam and what are the costs?

The fastest and cheapest way to get to Zaandam from Amsterdam is by train. The price for an one way ticket is 2.90€ .

The train journey time between Amsterdam Centraal Station and Zaandam is around 12 min, and the trains are leaving from Amsterdam Centraal Station at a maximum interval of 15 min.

Operated by Dutch Railways (NS), the Amsterdam Centraal Station to Zaandam train service departs from Amsterdam Centraal and arrives in Zaandam. According to NS 821 trains run weekly, although weekend and holiday schedules can vary so check in advance.
